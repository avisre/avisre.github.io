About

A little about me

I’m a full-stack developer who likes shipping small, complete things — a working app you can hand to a user, not a PowerPoint deck. Over the past six months I’ve shipped four products solo: a production fintech dashboard (Stock Portfolio Pro), a Flutter game on the Play Store (The Ledger Report), an on-device AI chat app for Android using Gemma 3n via LiteRT (Localyze), and an Android prayer app (Praarthana).

Before going independent I was Lead Web Developer at MeOmics in the UK, where I owned a full WordPress → modern JavaScript migration end-to-end. MA Digital Media Design from Birkbeck, University of London.

I’m now based in Trivandrum and open to remote contract work — fintech and data-viz specialism preferred. I like working with small teams that ship fast and care about craft. If that sounds like you, get in touch.

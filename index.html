About

I ship small, complete things.

I’m a cross-platform full-stack developer based in Trivandrum, India. I like shipping small, complete things — a working product you can hand to a user, not a slide deck. Over the last six months I’ve taken five products to production, solo and end-to-end: a fintech dashboard, an on-device AI chat app, a Flutter strategy game, a multilingual prayer app, and PlayShip — a productized Google Play submission service.

Previously I was Lead Web Developer at MeOmics (UK), where I led a full WordPress → modern JavaScript redevelopment — owning architecture, delivery, and release cycles (Apr 2024 – Jan 2025). I hold an MA in Digital Media Design from Birkbeck, University of London. After my UK Graduate visa term ended I returned to India, and I now build independently.

On the side I’ve gotten Gemma-class LLMs running on Snapdragon NPUs — including the “unsupported” 8 Gen 1 (Hexagon v69) — verified at ~31 tok/s on a OnePlus 10 Pro. I’m open to remote contract work, with a fintech and data-visualization specialism. If that fits what you’re building, let’s talk.

Featured · Fintech web app

Stock Portfolio Pro

A production fintech dashboard. Node.js + Express, MongoDB Atlas, and vanilla JS with custom D3.js v7 SVG charts. Live market data via yahoo-finance2 with an Alpha Vantage fallback, JWT plus Google/Facebook OAuth, and Stripe-gated paid tiers. A tiered TTL cache (60s quotes → 12h fundamentals) and a ~2.5 req/s outbound throttle keep it fast and within rate limits.
